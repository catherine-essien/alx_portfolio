The Blue House

An app designed to revolutionize learning experiences. The Blue House encompasses cutting-edge technologies, including AR glasses that create immersive language learning experiences by overlaying real-world objects with translations. Embracing sustainability, the Blue House features solar-powered e-book libraries, providing a vast digital resource hub that can be accessed and charged using renewable energy sources. By combining these innovative elements, the Blue House aims to redefine education, bridge the digital gap, and create an inclusive and environmentally conscious learning environment for students of diverse backgrounds.
